great white attack reported in australia!

a huge great white has attacked three adults and two kids and only one kid survived...

a family had gone on a holiday to australia this summer and only one of them is coming back...on saturday evening a family decided to go out on their hired yacht, it was the Smith family.Mum, dad, two kids and their aunty.As it was getting dark on of the kids reported seeing a shark in the water as the rest of the family was swimming.The shark started off attacking the auntie and then moved onto the rest of the family, the other child was attcked but barely survived and is now in hospital but their isnt much chance of survival..the child that remained on the boat quickly phoned the rescue service and he was taken away along with his brother, we are still awaiting more information that we will share as soon as possible.

the kid who survived (ben) has said "i didnt know how to help them so i called the rescue people!" his family have also opened a fund for people willing to donate to try and help ben through his grief. he has also been invited to share his story for shark awareness month next year.
